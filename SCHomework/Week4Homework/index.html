Things Will Get Better

Just Hold On

Important Things To Remember

  • Emotions are ever-changing
    • The sadness won't last forever.
    • Feelings can pass through! Like clouds
  • Just like the sad days come, good days will come as well
    • Focus on the good day around the corner
    • Trust that a good day will come just like that sad day came
    • Accept that sad day will happen but be excited about the upcoming good day
  • Use past happy moments to help you through the sad ones
  • Connecting with nature can do wonders!
    • Get some sun
    • Take a walk
    • Take care of a plant
  • Have a Game Plan
    • Accept that sad days will come but be prepared
    • Take note of the things that make your sad days a little less worse and implement them
    • Journal
  • Don't be afraid to reach out for help

Some Facts

  1. You are stronger than you think
  2. You are loved
  3. You are here for a reason

Again...Don't Be Afraid To Reach Out

Reaching out for help is never a sign of weakness! In fact, you're extremely brave for acknowledging that you need help and asking for it
